ABOUT

Type Jawns.

Type Media is a decentralized media organization built from the ground up inside the Doginal Dogs ecosystem. What started in February 2025 as a small content subcommittee has grown into something much bigger: a 24/7 media network powered entirely by its community.

We run live X Spaces around the clock. Not a few hours a day. Not when we feel like it. Every single hour of every single day, there is a Type Media or CSN-affiliated space live on the timeline. Our hosts span multiple time zones and bring everything from crypto alpha and market analysis to open mic conversations, music sessions, and late-night vibes.

Our affiliates are more than just members. They are badged creators, builders, and voices who represent the brand across X and beyond. Each affiliate earns their badge through consistent contribution, whether that means hosting spaces, creating content, building tools, or simply showing up and pushing the culture forward every day.

Type Media operates with a council-driven governance model. Seven council members guide the direction of the organization, making decisions on partnerships, treasury management, new initiatives, and the future of the brand. The council holds Doginal Dogs NFTs in the treasury and actively participates in the broader Doginal Dogs ecosystem.

At our core, we believe that community-owned media is the future. No corporate sponsors dictating the narrative. No algorithms deciding who gets heard. Just real people, real conversations, and real culture being built in real time. Type Media is proof that a decentralized group of passionate individuals can build something that rivals traditional media networks in reach, consistency, and impact.
